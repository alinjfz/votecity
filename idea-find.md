## Prize Tracks — Criteria & Requirements

You can aim at **multiple tracks** with one project if it fits.

### Track 1: Bolt.new (Technical Execution)

**Prizes:** $1,000 (1st) · $750 (2nd) · $500 (Best Collaborative) + 1 year Bolt Pro each

**What they judge:**
- Quality of the **Bolt.new build** — real app/website/tool
- Use of Bolt features (Plan mode, Share, integrations, APIs)
- **Working demo** via published link
- Novel integrations or API connections (bonus for technical builders)

**Best Collaborative prize — extra requirements:**
- **4 or more collaborators**
- Better if collaborators share the **same company email domain**

**Bolt.new tips from organisers:**
- Use **Plan mode** to discuss ideas before building
- Use **Share** to invite collaborators
- Explore Bolt's ready-made partner platform integrations
- Build custom API connections if technical

---

### Track 2: SilkFlo (Business Value / ROI)

**Prizes:** £500 (1st) · £300 (2nd) · £200 (3rd) + SilkFlo Pro · Trophy for Highest Value AI Idea · AI Value Audit (£15k value) for 1st

**What they judge:**
- **Quantifiable business value** — ROI forecast generated by SilkFlo's AI
- **Detailed use case description** with real numbers
- Idea ranked on **live leaderboard** (Pipeline → All) by potential ROI
- Project idea in SilkFlo **must match** the Bolt.new submission

**SilkFlo submission — what to include (the more detail, the better ROI forecast):**
- Who does the work (team size, roles)
- What process is being automated/improved
- Volume (transactions, documents, tasks per day/week)
- Time per task (minutes/hours)
- Current costs (staff time, vendor fees, error rates, rework)
- Expected savings from AI automation

**Example use case (from organisers):**
> "Our back-office team processes trade confirmations and settlement instructions. We have about 20 operations staff handling roughly 500 confirmations per day, each taking around 12 minutes. We're also paying about £95,000 a year to a third-party reconciliation vendor because our internal process generates too many breaks. AI automation should reduce both the manual processing time and eliminate most of the reconciliation vendor cost."

**SilkFlo process:**
1. Sign up via hackathon invite link
2. On Personal Dashboard, describe AI use case in detail
3. Click **Generate Idea**
4. View your ranking on leaderboard

**Idea fit for SilkFlo:** Ops automation, finance back-office, claims, compliance, reconciliation, document processing, customer service at scale — anything with clear £/$ savings.

## Core Judging Themes (Across All Tracks)

When brainstorming, ideas should score well on at least 2–3 of these:

1. **Valuable AI** — solves a real problem, not a toy demo
2. **Positive impact** — measurably improves life or work
3. **Collaboration** — team build, especially same-company teams (4+)
4. **Execution** — polished working demo in 48 hours
5. **Business case** — quantifiable ROI (critical for SilkFlo)
6. **Creativity** — fresh angle, unexpected application
7. **Integrations** — connects to real tools/APIs/platforms
8. **Social engagement** — visible build journey on social media

## Scope Rules for 48-Hour Builds

- **One core workflow** — not a platform with 10 features
- **Demo > completeness** — one polished flow beats half-built product
- **Use Bolt integrations** — faster than custom backend
- **Plan first** — use Bolt Plan mode before coding
- **Match SilkFlo early** — write the business case before or during build so numbers align with demo

## Idea Brainstorming Prompts

Use these to generate candidates:

1. What repetitive task do you or someone you know do daily that takes 10+ minutes?
2. What vendor/software cost could be reduced with AI automation?
3. What document type gets read/reviewed manually at scale?
4. What decision gets delayed because someone needs to synthesise information?
5. What non-profit or community problem could a simple app solve?
6. What Bolt integration (email, calendar, CRM, etc.) could power a one-click workflow?
7. What would impress a CFO if you showed ROI in year 1?
8. What would make a great 30-second demo video for social media?

---

## Anti-Patterns — Ideas to Avoid

- Pre-existing projects rebranded for the hackathon
- Ideas with no clear demo (too abstract, no UI)
- Full platforms (CRM, ERP, marketplace) — too big for 48h
- Ideas with no measurable value story (weak for SilkFlo)
- Pure chatbots with no specific workflow or integration
- Copying obvious templates without a twist (hard to win SLG creativity)
- Building without submitting SilkFlo case if aiming at that track

---

## Recommended Brainstorming Workflow

1. **Brain dump** 10–15 ideas using prompts above
2. **Score** each on the evaluation scorecard
3. **Shortlist** top 3
4. **Draft SilkFlo case** for each shortlisted idea (test which ROI story is strongest)
5. **Reality-check** build scope — can you demo the core flow in Bolt.new in ~24h?
6. **Pick one** — commit, Plan mode in Bolt, build, submit SilkFlo + portal link
